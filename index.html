GAME MECHANICS
  1. Chain Reacts is a 2-player game with 2 different types of atoms to be placed in regions of a chamber. Each player controls controls an atom of a specific color, with each player placing an atom in their cell at their turn, through clicking their desired cell.
  2. Players have an “Undo” option which allows them to undo their last move and correct the placement of their atom, given that the opposing player hasn’t made their move yet (however, we made it so that you can undo however many amount of moves you want to undo.)
  3. Each cell in the chamber has their own capacity. The cells in the corner are most reactive, upon placing 2 atoms on the corner cells, they fuse together, exploding and reacting to the atoms in adjacent cell, converting them to their own color. Similarly, the atoms along the lengths and widths of the chamber require 3 atoms before fusing, and the rest of the cells require 4 cells to fuse.
  4. The explosion caused by fusion could also trigger the fusion and explosion of atoms in other adjacent cells, causing a CHAIN REACTION.
  5. A player wins once all the atoms in the cells of the chamber are of that his/her/their color.
